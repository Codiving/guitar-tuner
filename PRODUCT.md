# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Anyone who wants to tune a guitar quickly and without friction, on desktop or mobile, in a practice room, at home, or before a session.

## Product Purpose

Provide a simple, reliable guitar tuner that detects pitch, shows the current note and cents offset, and helps the user get in tune with minimal setup.

## Brand Personality

Clean, calm, direct.

The interface should feel uncluttered, trustworthy, and easy to understand at a glance.

## Anti-references

Overdesigned music apps, cluttered dashboards, flashy gradients, decorative motion, and interfaces that make simple tuning feel complicated.

## Design Principles

- Keep the primary tuning loop obvious and low-friction.
- Prefer familiar controls and clear feedback over novelty.
- Make state changes easy to scan without visual noise.
- Use restrained visuals and consistent component behavior.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for strong contrast, large tap targets, keyboard access, and reduced-motion friendly interactions. Feedback should remain understandable without relying on color alone.
